James' Top Six Films

The movies that I've thoroughly enjoyed throughout my life

Terminator 2

Pure 90s action/sci-fi magic. Directed by James Cameron, starring Arnie.

The Crow

Another 90s classic overshadowed by the tragic end of the star, Brandon Lee.

La Vita e Bella

Roberto Benigni stars alongside Nicoletta Braschi in this legendary tragic comedy.

Big Fish

A Tim Burton masterpiece which questions the limits of artistic license and sees a frustrated son reconcile his relationship with his dying father.

Twin Romance

Tarantino's first movie. Christian Slater and Patricia Arquette lead an absolutely flawless cast including; Gary Oldman, Dennis Hopper, Brad Pitt and many more.

Fear & Loathing in Las Vegas

Hunter S Thompson's legendary work was brought to life by Terry Gilliam. Johnny Depp and Benicio del Toro take the wheel across bat country.
